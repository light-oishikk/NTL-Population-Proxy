NTL Population Proxy Mapping — Research Package

April 2026 | Oishik Kar | IIIT Dharwad

This is original research work. All experiments, code, and results were generated independently.

Key points:

Study covers 670 Indian districts and 695 Nigerian LGAs using VIIRS VNL V2.1 (2019) and WorldPop 2019

Main finding: urban rural stratification via KMeans on NTL features improves R² significantly in both countries (India p=0.027, Nigeria p=0.004), validated with 5 fold CV and paired t tests

Size confound explicitly tested and ruled out (r=0.119)

NDVI tested and found harmful in tropical dispersed settlement regions (Kerala Effect)

Bangladesh failed due to insufficient sample size (n=64) and is documented as a methodological boundary case
